1. The basics

  1. Business name — exactly as you want it displayed
  2. Tagline — one short line (e.g. "Licensed plumber, 100% satisfaction guarantee — Salt Lake's straightforward plumbing call")
  3. Phone number(s) — main line + emergency line if different
  4. Email for prospect inquiries
  5. Service area — cities you cover (e.g. "Provo, Orem, Lehi, Pleasant Grove")
  6. Business hours — or "by appointment" / "24/7 emergency"
  7. License / insured — any state DOPL or contractor numbers you want displayed

2. Services (or menu, if restaurant)

5–8 things you do, with a one-sentence description for each. Price ranges welcome but not required. Example for plumbing:

Emergency Plumbing — Burst pipes, sewer backups, no hot water. Same-day dispatch. (From $149)
Water Heater Service — Tank, tankless, electric, gas. Repair, replacement, new install. (From $349)
Drain & Sewer Cleaning — Clogged sink, slow tub, backed-up main line. (From $149)

3. Photos

3–10 photos. Mix is best:

  • Exterior / storefront / truck

Send via email attachment, Google Drive / Dropbox link, or Instagram handle (I'll pull from there).

4. Reviews / testimonials

3 real quotes from real customers. If you don't have any written, send me a link to your Google / Yelp / BBB reviews and I'll pull the best ones.
